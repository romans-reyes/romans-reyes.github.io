Tom Yum Goong: A Spicy Thai Delight

Tom Yum Goong is a Thai soup renowned for it's bold flavors and aromantic spices. This hot and sour soup features a tantalizing blend of lemongrass, galangal, and Kaffir lime leaves, combined with succulent shrimp.

"The to a delicious Tom Yum Goong lies in the balance of flavors sweet, salt, sour, and spicy."

Ingredients:

  • lemongrass: 2 stalks
  • Thai basil: 1 cup
  • kaffir lime leaves: 3 leaves
  • shrimp: 500kg

preparation:

  1. Simmer the broth for 10 minutes.
    2. ^
  2. Add lemongrass, galangal, and Kaffir lime leaves.
  3. Stir in shrimp andcookuntil pink.
  4. Season with sauce, lime juice, and chili paste.
  5. Garnish with Thai basil and serve hot

Cooking Tips:

  • 1/4 cup of fish sauce add authentic thai flavor
  • 1 tablespoon of chili paste gives the soup it signature heat.
